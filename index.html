🐠 What's New

A little aquarium that keeps growing.

🐢 Death and turtles · Jun 5, 2026

  • A sea turtle now drifts through the tank. Just like the fish, it makes friends and enemies (tap it to see them) — but it never has babies.
  • The turtle sometimes eats its enemies — and only its enemies — when it bumps into one, with a snappy little chomp.
  • 🦀 Crabs can climb aboard the turtle's shell and ride along — and sometimes, if the turtle's swimming high enough, they'll base-jump off and parachute down in a pair of cool shades. 😎
  • 💥 A fish that gets too hungry now bursts into a puff of food for the others.

🦀 Crab rave · Jun 5, 2026

  • Poke a crab 6 times in a row to throw a crab rave — disco lights, a thumping beat, and crabs dancing in unison.
  • You can now keep up to 100 crabs in the tank.
  • The menu bar moved to the top so it stays out of the crabs' way.

Friends, family & rivalries · Jun 5, 2026

  • Fish make up to 5 friends and gently swim toward them.
  • Fish never befriend or have babies with close family (parents, children, or siblings) — but cousins are fair game.
  • Every fish now has a hunger meter that rises over time and refills when it eats.
  • A hungry fish can turn into an enemy of one that eats right in front of it (up to 3). Enemies swim away from each other.

More fish variety · Jun 5, 2026

  • New inherited traits: ✨ glow, 🫧 bubbles, 🦄 horn, 🐱 whiskers, 👁️ eyelashes.
  • Glowing fish shine much brighter at night.

Day & night · Jun 4, 2026

  • The tank drifts through day and night, with stars and a moon after dark.
  • 🌗 button to choose: cycle, always day, or always night.
  • A sparkly patch of surface light appears now and then and tempts fish upward.

A growing garden · Jun 4, 2026

  • 🌱 button plants seaweed that sprouts from a seed and grows tall.

Meet the fish · Jun 4, 2026

  • Every fish has a name and an age (1 year per minute).
  • Tap a fish to see its info, friends and family.
  • Fish grow a little bigger each time they eat.

Hello, tank! · Jun 4, 2026

  • Fish, octopuses, crabs, seahorses, castles, bubbles, and surprise whales & jellyfish.
